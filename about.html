About me

A mathematician and systems engineer passionately for programming, literature, and well-living things.

My name is Hamilton Gonçalves Junior. I graduated in Mathematics in the 1990s and in Information Systems now in 2018. I was always in love with Computing at the beginning as a hobby. I always liked Classical Literature. And when you learn a lot of things, you become smarter and start liking to live well and all that is related.

I studied mathematics at the University of São Paulo, São Carlos campus, at the Institute of Mathematical and Computer Sciences. I worked in many places later, in banks, hotels and schools. After being mature, I decided to study Information Systems at the same institute. I met old friends and it was very good.

Now I'm looking for a job. I work as a freelancer. I have interest in Java, Python and programming for Android. But I like web programming too (I like all about programming). I am currently studying these things and developing some of what I have already learned.

From the classics of Brazilian Literature and World Literature I've read a lot. I'm slowly rereading some important works and I have many others to read. I have occupation for life, certainly.

About well-being, well, I'm living. I try to keep my friendships up to date and make new ones, always worried about the quality of these relationships. I'm a little lazy to do exercises, but I know they're important and that's why I do it anyway. I have made a lighter diet and made breaks during working hours. It is a tendency today for people to take care of body and soul: I am getting more involved in this tendency. Anyway, I'm a happy person.
