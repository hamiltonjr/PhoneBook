Araçatuba cool places to go

Araçatuba is a medium-sized city of the State of São Paulo far from São Paulo city. These cities are so well known as places where there is not much cool to do. But if we look for, we find the best places to suit all tastes.

Downtown

Visiting the downtown, go shopping, meeting shops and bars is fun for many tastes. The shops are open during the day and the bar opens when the night begins and the fun goes all niht long.

Praça Nova Shopping Mall

Going shopping and enjoying great diners and restaurants is guaranteed fun. There are movies, video games, McDonalds, etc.

Hot Planet Thermas Park

It has hot pools, water games, snack bars and lots of beautiful people. Its a hot point for fun of all families.
